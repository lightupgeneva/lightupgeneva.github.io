LightUpGeneva Hosted by Jack & Crystal on 89.9 WGSG Radio 4 Making Memories.

LightUpGeneva is a computer controlled animated light display featuring 640 channels controlling over 140,000 lights, synchronized to music which is broadcast over 89.9 radio. Technical Details

Jack and Crystal are our animated snowmen that are the announcers and news correspondent who tell the history of the candy cane and the Christmas tree and other traditions while telling a few jokes. About the Display

GSG 4 Making Memories stands for "God’s Sustaining Gifts" and is the name of the companies that we created while developing wind farms in Illinois. Why We Do It

  • Why That Holiday Glow Might Look Chillier This Year
    Wall Street Journal

    Bruce Papiech, a wind-farm developer in Geneva, Ill., went all-LED this season in his most elaborate holiday display ever: an extravaganza of more than 100,000 shimmering lights, choreographed via computer to Christmas carols.Several contractors assembled the display over seven weeks.

    "I can literally plug everything into a single outlet," says Mr. Papiech, 59 years old, who estimated he is using less than 20 amps of electricity compared to the 100 he would need with incandescent lights.
